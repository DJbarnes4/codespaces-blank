Black Panthers

A black panther is the melanistic colour variant of the leopard (Panthera pardus) and the jaguar (Panthera onca). Black panthers of both species have excess black pigments, but their typical rosettes are also present.

  • Fun Facts
    1. Mostly in tropical forests in Africa, Asia, and South America
    2. Black Panthers are great swimmers
    3. Black Panthers can weigh up to 300 pounds
    4. Black Panthers climb trees for their advantage
  • Types of Black Panthers
    1. jaguar
    2. Leopard
